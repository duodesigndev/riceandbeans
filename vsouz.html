About

Spacenow are Pioneers of the Activated Space Economy. We activate a near limitless range of underutilised spaces on the most trusted platform to drive growth and opportunity. Spacenow unlike any other collaborative space or service provider is a truly mega platform. We redefine space, any space and make it available for others. More than sharing, we provide access to monetise that which you don’t use and provide the exchange to acquire that which you don’t. Spacenow is the world’s first space activation economy because we are activating all space for the benefit of individuals. Welcome to the new era of Activated-Spaces.

The Problem

The Solution

Spacenow plans to streamline the entire short term rental process using blockchain and smart contract technology to eliminate the need for a middleman, making it possible for guests and hosts to complete all leasing tasks in one place.

Spacenow addresses a global issue in commerce: the too small to consider. There is a massive amount of real estate and asset categories left idle and not generating income for the asset holder because of the time and effort involved. Spacenow provides the solution with its short term rental model, which includes hourly, daily, weekly, monthly or longer-term booking. Spacenow’s other services such as VR, keyless entry and the secure payment system also eliminate the need for inspection.

Whitepaper

Spacenow is challenging the definition of commercial property by providing an online exchange to monetise a variety of underutilized spaces in completely new ways. We use blockchain technology to facilitate smart contracts between guests and hosts thereby decentralising the process of renting a space.

Our hosts can reimagine how they see their space, redefine what their space can be used for, and then connect with potential guests who may wish to rent it. Owners can monetise any whole dormant space, or capitalise on bringing a part of their space to life by offering it on our platform.
